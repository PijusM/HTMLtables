Table Cells

Each cell is defined by a <td> and </td> tag.

td stands for table data.

Everything between and are the content of the table cell.

Example

<table>
 <tr>
  <th>Course Name</th>
  <th>Course Tutor</th>
  <th>Price</th>
 </tr>
 <tr>
  <td>After the Civil War</td>
  <td>Mark Cottle</td>
  <td>$25</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
  <td>An introduction to Anglo-Saxon England</td>
  <td>Dr. John Wroughton</td>
  <td>$32</td>
 </tr>
</table>

Displays:

Course Name Course Tutor Price
After the Civil War Mark Cottle $25
An introduction to Anglo-Saxon England Dr. John Wroughton $32
